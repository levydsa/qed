  - <Question>
    $$
      \lim_{x \to 0} \left[1 + \cos\left(\frac{ π + x^2}{3}\right)\right]
    $$
    </Question>
  - <Question>
    $$
      \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{10x^7 + 4x + 1}{3 + 5x^7}

    $$
